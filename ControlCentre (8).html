Four Design Principles — Test Every Build
1Does it make sense?
2Can we make it more efficient?
3Does it feel intuitive?
4What phase does this belong to — and are we in that phase yet?
Five Pillars — Strategic Framework
1Where am I now?
2Where do I want to be?
3How do I get there?
4Which way is best?
5How do I ensure arrival?
