Four Design Principles — Test Every Build
1Does it make sense?
2Can we make it more efficient?
3Does it feel intuitive?
4What phase does this belong to — and are we in that phase yet?
Five Pillars — Strategic Framework
1Where am I now?
2Where do I want to be?
3How do I get there?
4Which way is best?
5How do I ensure arrival?
Wilson, R.M.S. & Gilligan, C. — Strategic Marketing Management (3rd Ed., c.2000)
Four Ps — Marketing Mix
PProduct — what's being offered
PPrice — what it costs, verified against reality
PPlace — where and how it reaches the customer
PPromotion — how people find out about it
